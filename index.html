The Day's Journey

Saturday, August 15, 2026

Dress Code: Formal / Cocktail Attire

2:30 PM

Wedding Ceremony

Holy Trinity Church
1035 Ellis St E, Windsor, ON N8X 2J2, Canada Witness the beginning of our forever in a traditional ceremony (approx. 45 minutes).

3:45 PM

The Intermission

Take a breather before the celebration continues and explore the area. Since we're already downtown, we highly recommend a quick detour to the Windsor Waterfront for a stroll with iconic views of the Detroit skyline.

6:00 PM

Cocktail Hour

Kingscoast Estate Winery
1000 McCain Side Rd, Kingsville, ON N9Y 2E6, Canada Doors open! Join us for drinks and appetizers before the evening begins.

7:30 PM

Dinner

Settle in for a wonderful evening of dinner and heartfelt speeches accompanied by live music featuring Windsor's finest, The Wedding Belles.

8:30 PM

Coffee & Gelato Bar

Enjoy authentic Italian gelato and espresso served from charming specialty carts, lovingly prepared by Windsor's own family-run Sottozero Catering.

10:30 PM

Late Night Snack

Keep the energy up on the dance floor! Refuel with fresh wood-oven baked pizza and an assortment of sweets from our full dessert table.

1:00 AM

End of Night

Our incredible night comes to a close. Thank you for the laughter, the dances, and the memories that will last us a lifetime. We love you all!
